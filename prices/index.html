FEES WILL BE REVIEWED ANNUALLY.

The Nursery has obtained ‘The Cute Fruit Plus Award’ ensuring that all snack, meals and drinks provided are healthy. We can cater to all dietary needs.

The fees for full time and daily include:

  • Free choice breakfast served until 9.00
  • Morning snack
  • Dinner
  • Afternoon snack
  • Hot tea for the under two’s
  • Wipes/ cotton wool

The fees for morning sessions include:

  • Free choice breakfast served until 9.00
  • Morning snack
  • Dinner
  • Wipes/ cotton wool

The fees for afternoon sessions include; -Afternoon snack -Warm tea for the under two’s -Wipes/ cotton wool " Parents/carers provide Nappies, spare clothes and formula milk (where applicable)

The nursery will closes for approximately one week at Christmas – The dates will be confirmed nearer to the time. There will be no charge for this period.

The nursery will close on ALL BANK HOLIDAYS Full Fees will apply.

Two weeks annual holiday will be charged at half rate.

Sickness, and any other absenteeism over and above this will be charged at full rate. This enables your place to be retained.

If paying weekly FEES MUST BE PAID ON THE FIRST DAY YOUR CHILD ATTENDS NURSERY. Late payments will be charged a fee please see LATE PAYMENT POLICY. Monthly payment is payable in advance. Payment is by standing order or cash only, no cheques will be accepted.

In the event of non-payment of fees, we reserve the right to exclude your child from the Nursery temporarily and ultimately terminate your contract.

Where children from one family BOTH attend FULL TIME there will be a 5% discount for the eldest child.

We are in receipt of nursery education funding for two, three and four year olds, please speak to a member of staff for details. We accept Childcare Vouchers please speak to Suzanne with regards Childcare Vouchers Provider number. We are also signed up to the tax-free childcare scheme, if you would like more information on this please speak to a member of staff.

Children must be collected PROMPTLY from the Nursery at the end of each session. If you collect your child later than the specified time, there will be a charge of £5.00per ¼ or part ¼ hour.

If your child is unable to attend Nursery due to sickness or otherwise please telephone before 10.00 am.

If you wish to change a regular booking then please give us a minimum of two weeks notice so that we can do our best to fill your cancelled booking and ensure that there is a place in the session that you require to change to. Although we do our best to be flexible and accommodate your request unfortunately we can’t guarantee this, as it will depend on our bookings and staff for that session.

Any additional sessions booked in advance and agreed on will be charged at full rate unless 24 hours notice has been given.
