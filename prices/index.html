Our Aims

  • Provide high quality care and education for children below statutory school age;
  • Embrace the new Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) and use it as the basis of our approach to early learning and development;
  • Work in partnership with parents to help children to learn and develop;
  • Ensure that each child is in a safe and stimulating environment;
  • Ensure that all children can fulfill their potential;
  • Offer children and their parents a service that promotes equality and values diversity.
